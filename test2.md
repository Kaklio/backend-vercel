**Definition** – "Casualties of the Iranian Revolution" refers to all deaths that occurred during the Iranian Revolution (January 1978 – February 1979) and its immediate aftermath, including protestors, political prisoners, and people executed by the new Islamic Republic.

### 1.1 Divergent death-toll estimates

| Source / Claim | Estimated deaths | Notes / Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
