### 2. Trailer Highlights (State of Play)  
- The trailer showcased **The Great Hollow** opening in Limveld.  
- Introduced the **Undertaker** and **Scholar** Nightfarers.  
- Displayed two **Third Day** bosses, one resembling a **mass of women**.  
- Showed **field boss Artorias** from *Dark Souls*.  
- Voice‑over hints at post‑Long‑Night events.  

*No further lore details were provided; fans are expected to wait for post‑release lore videos.*

### 3. About **Elden Ring: Nightreign**  
- **Genre:** Multiplayer co‑op riff on *Elden Ring* (1‑3 players).  
- **Core Loop:** Three nights of exploration, loot hunting, and boss battles against **Night Lords**.  
- **Launch:** **May 2024**.  
- **Initial Review:** **7/10** – praised as a strong three‑player co‑op experience, though solo play felt unbalanced.  
  - Review link: [/elden-ring-nightreign/239300/review/elden-ring-nightreign-review](/elden-ring-nightreign/239300/review/elden-ring-nightreign-review)
