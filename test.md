**Lahore’s Bird Market Demolition Sparks Controversy, Public Outcry**  
*Published Nov 7 2025 – Dawn*  
Author: **Khalid Hasnain** – senior journalist (with Dawn since 2007) – Twitter: [@KhalidHasnain10](https://twitter.com/KhalidHasnain10) – Facebook: Khalid.Hasnain.7  

---  

## 1. Event Overview  

- **Location:** Bhati Chowk area, Lahore (near Data Darbar).  
- **Date of operation:** Wednesday morning, Nov 2025.  
- **Executing agencies:**  
  - Lahore Development Authority (LDA)  
  - Traffic Engineering and Transport Planning Agency (TEPA)  
- **Purpose of demolition:** Part of the **Bhaati Chowk remodeling project** aimed at reducing traffic congestion and controlling pollution.  

> “The market consisted of a total of 149 shops, including some tuck (pan) shops. Around 13 to 14 [shops] were situated in the front, where the shopkeepers were able to take out the birds and cages late at night. The other shopkeepers could not do this since they closed the shops in the evening as per routine,” – **Bahu Khan**, Lahore Bhati Bird Association office bearer (as quoted by Dawn).  

---  

## 2. Shopkeepers’ Allegations  

| Claim | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Number of shops** | 149 total. |
| **Bird evacuation** | Only 13‑14 front‑facing shops could move birds/cages late at night; the rest closed in the evening and could not evacuate. |
| **Outcome for birds** | “All birds (parrots, sparrows, etc.) were buried to death after the field‑operation teams raised all structures with heavy machinery.” – Bahu Khan. |
| **Economic loss** | Birds and other caged pets valued at **millions of rupees** reportedly killed. |
| **Source of claim** | Direct interview with Dawn. |

---  

## 3. Government / LDA Response  

- **Official spokesperson:** LDA Director‑General **Tahir Farooq** (telephonic interview with Dawn).  
- **Key statements:**  

  1. “All shopkeepers were aware of this operation already, and our teams also helped them shift birds and other goods to other places.”  
  2. “The pictures being shown on the media are fake, as not a single bird was injured.”  
  3. Described the allegations as “propaganda by the shopkeepers to defame the government” and suggested “some political elements are running this campaign against the government.”  

- **Position:** **Denial** of any bird deaths; asserts that the demolition was conducted with notice and assistance.
