## Grand Theft Auto VI – Release Announcement  

**Source:** Rockstar Games Newswire (published **November 6, 2025**)  

### Key Announcement  

- **Release Date:** Thursday, **November 19, 2026**.  
- **Statement from Rockstar Games:**  

  > “We are sorry for adding additional time to what we realize has been a long wait, but these extra months will allow us to finish the game with the level of polish you have come to expect and deserve.  
  > We want to thank you again for your patience and support. While the wait is a little longer, we are incredibly excited for players to experience the sprawling state of **Leonida** and a return to modern‑day **Vice City**.”  

- **Sign‑off:** *Sincerely, Rockstar Games*  

### Context & Rationale  

- The delay adds **several months** to the originally anticipated launch window.  
- Purpose of the extension: ensure **high‑quality polish** and meet player expectations.  
- Highlights of the upcoming game world:  
  - A new, expansive setting named **Leonida**.  
  - A revisited version of **Vice City** set in the modern era.
