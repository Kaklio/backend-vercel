### 2. Appearance  

- Young man with light‑colored hair and an unusually long chin.  
- Attire: frilly white shirt, black pants.  
- Constantly holds a **rose in his mouth**.  

---

### 3. Personality  

- True to his name, **showy, arrogant, and flamboyant**.  
- Deep admiration for **[Sweet Mask](/wiki/Sweet_Mask "Sweet Mask")**; during the Monster Association raid he asked Sweet Mask to “pay attention to his fighting style.”  

---

### 4. Plot involvement  

#### 4.1 Human Monster Saga  

##### 4.1.1 Super Fight Arc  
- Appears in **I‑City** to aid A‑Class heroes against **[Hundred‑Eyes Octopus](/wiki/Hundred-Eyes_Octopus "Hundred-Eyes Octopus")**【1】.  

##### 4.1.2 Monster Association Arc  
- Recruited for the **Monster Association** raid support team【2】.  
- While fighting, asks Sweet Mask to watch his “beautiful fighting style.”  
- Deflects a monster’s attack with a rapid series of punches, then grabs another monster’s neck and knees it, killing it.  
- Sweet Mask compliments his “potential,” actually referring to **[Feather](/wiki/Feather "Feather")** who was fighting behind Narcisstoic【3】.  
- Joins the attack on **[Rhino Wrestler](/wiki/Rhino_Wrestler "Rhino Wrestler")**, striking with his **Rose American Sack**, but is easily knocked back; Rhino Wrestler rates his attack **18/100**【4】.  
- The support team later escorts **[Waganma](/wiki/Waganma "Waganma")** to safety; **Nyan** appears and incapacitates several heroes, including Narcisstoic.  

---

### 5. Abilities and Powers  

#### 5.1 Physical Abilities  

- **Enhanced Strength:** Superhuman strength; can crush monsters with bare fists and wield cinder‑blocks or street signs.  
- **Enhanced Speed:** High agility; can execute his signature **Soft Touch Execution** while tossing a rose and catching it mid‑air.  

#### 5.2 Fighting Style  

- **Hand‑to‑Hand Combatant** with an “elegant” (ironic) style.  

| Technique | Japanese name | Description |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| **Soft Touch Execution** | ソフトタッチエクスキューション (*Sofutotacchiekusukyūshon*) | Brutal barrage of punches, knee strikes, and improvised weapons (e.g., cinder‑blocks). Eliminated three monsters in rapid succession; capable of tossing a rose into the air and catching it before it lands【5】. |
| **Rose American Sack** (aka **Rose Brass Knuckles**) | ローズメリケンサーック (*Rōzumerikensākku*) | Uses brass knuckles fashioned like a rose; noted in Chapter 96【6】. |

---

### 6. Trivia  

- **Name origin:** Portmanteau of **“Narcissist”** (ナルシスト) + **“Stoic”** (ストイック).
